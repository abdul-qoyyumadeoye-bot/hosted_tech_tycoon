TechTycoon Simulator

Management Sim

Build the startup. Survive the sprint.

Lead a tech team through deadlines, crises, trade-offs, and launch pressure.

Run Length 20-30 min
Play Style Team Strategy
Core Loop Choose, react, recover

In TechTycoon, your team runs a startup under real pressure. Every stage costs time, money, and stakeholder confidence.

You will balance four live metrics throughout the run:

  • Impact: are you solving the real problem?
  • Inclusivity: who benefits, and who gets left out?
  • Trust: do users and schools still believe in you?
  • Budget: how much runway do you have left?
